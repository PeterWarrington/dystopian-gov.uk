About Dystopian GOV.UK.

18th January 2022


I have created this website as a response to the UK Government's increasingly worrying policies regarding our democracy, our policing and our media. These include:


  • The introduction of the Police, Crime, Sentencing and Courts Bill that will enable police to criminalize protesters by imposing restrictions such as noise and time limits, with the ability to stop-and-search anyone in the vicinity of a protest.
  • The removal of public funding for the BBC, Britain's public service broadcaster, in whole by 2027, plans that only seek to destroy a broadcaster which occasionally publishes news stories which the Government doesn't like.
  • The introduction of the Nationality and Borders Bill that will allow the UK Government to remove anyone's Citizenship without notice and effectively no opportunity of appeal, on the vague grounds that they are "conducive to the public good". It will also make it illegal to assist asylum-seekers in any way, even without making any gain.

Through this website, I have sought to portray the danger these policies and others pose, by portraying a society in which the freedoms the UK Government are currently trying to erode are gone for good.
