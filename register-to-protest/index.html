Register to protest Register to protest legally.

Using this service, you can register to legally protest quickly and easily online. You will need to provide:

  • Name
  • Address
  • Government Identity Number

Entering details of the protest is not required.

You will then be placed on a waiting list after which you can expect a response from a uniformed Home Office official within 24 hours.

! Warning Protesting without authorization by the Home Office can result in imprisonment without charge.

Registering takes around 5 minutes.
