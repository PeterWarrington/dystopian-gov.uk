Manage your Social Credit Score Manage your future in our Great country.

The Social Credit Score is a new and innovative system only used in this Great Country, Generously allowing Citizen's access to food, water and other luxuries based on their contribution's to our Great Nation. When you do something good for our nation, such as report a democratic traitor to the police force, your points go up, but if you do something bad such as use transportation vehicles at prohibited times, your points go down. Your points total is also influenced by the actions of those around you, such as family, friends and checkout assistants. Make sure to keep your close circle clean.

Have fun managing your score below!

A typical action managing your social credit score can take anywhere around 1 minute to 9 hours.
